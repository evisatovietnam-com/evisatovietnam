Types of Visas Available

1. Regular Tourist Visa

The regular tourist visa allows foreigners to enter Vietnam for tourism and visiting relatives. The duration of this visa is typically from 1 to 3 months and requires basic documents such as a passport valid for at least 6 months, a visa application form, and a passport photo.

2. Electronic Visa (e-visa)

The electronic visa (e-visa) is the latest visa type issued by Vietnam for citizens of over 80 countries. This is an online visa application process that requires simple procedures such as filling in information on the evisatovietnam system, paying the visa fee, and receiving the visa via email. E-visas usually have a validity period of up to 30 days for purposes like tourism, visiting relatives, or short-term business.

3. Visa On Arrival

Visa on Arrival is a traditional visa type that requires applicants to visit the Vietnam embassy or consulate in their home country to submit documents and visa application procedures. Processing time for this visa can take from 5 to 7 working days.

Necessary Requirements for evisatovietnam Visa Application

To apply for a visa via evisatovietnam, travelers need to prepare the following documents:

  • A passport valid for at least 6 months from the expected date of entry.
  • A completed visa application form.
  • A passport photo with a white background.
  • Payment of the visa fee through online payment methods such as credit card or bank transfer.

In addition to these basic requirements, travelers should be aware of specific regulations for each type of visa depending on its intended use.
